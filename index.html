Program Description

Kook (operated by Somerset Street Inc) offers a post-purchase SMS engagement program for customers who buy Kook products on Amazon. Messages may include order follow-ups, product satisfaction check-ins, review requests, discount codes, and customer support for product issues.

Order follow-up Product satisfaction check-in Review requests Discount codes Product support

How You Opt In

You opt in by scanning a QR code on a product insert card and texting the keyword KOOK to our number. By sending this message, you consent to receive text messages from Kook. A full walkthrough of the opt-in flow is available at our Opt-In Disclosure page.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. Typically 3–5 messages per conversation.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages.

Help

For help, reply HELP to any message or email support@shopkook.com.

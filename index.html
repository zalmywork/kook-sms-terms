What is the Kook SMS Program?

When you purchase a Kook product on Amazon, your order includes a product insert card with a QR code. Scanning the QR code lets you text us directly so we can follow up on your experience, offer support, and share exclusive offers. Participation is entirely voluntary.

How It Works

1
Find the insert card in your Kook product packaging. It includes a QR code and the message "Text us to share your experience and get a special thank you."
2
Scan the QR code with your phone's camera. It opens your messaging app with our number and a pre-filled keyword (e.g., Jar).
3
Press Send to start the conversation. No messages are sent to you until you text us first.
4
You'll receive a confirmation message and can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

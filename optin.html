Kook SMS Program — Opt-In Disclosure

How customers consent to receive text messages from Kook

The Product Insert Card

Every Kook product shipped through Amazon includes a physical insert card printed with a QR code. The card is the only way a customer joins the SMS program — there is no web form, no checkbox at checkout, and no pre-checked consent. The customer must physically scan the card and send a message first.

The Opt-In Flow, Step by Step

1
A physical product insert card is included in every Kook product order shipped via Amazon.
2
The customer voluntarily scans the QR code printed on the card.
3
Scanning opens the customer's native SMS / messaging app with Kook's phone number and the pre-filled keyword KOOK.
4
The customer must manually press Send to initiate the conversation. No messages are sent by Kook until the customer texts first.
5
After the customer sends the keyword, they receive an opt-in confirmation:
Kook: Thanks for reaching out! We'll follow up about your recent purchase. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help, STOP to opt out.

Disclosures

Message frequency
Message frequency varies based on customer interaction. Typically 3–5 messages per conversation.
Opt-out
Customers can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.
Help
Customers can reply HELP for support information.
Rates
Message and data rates may apply.
